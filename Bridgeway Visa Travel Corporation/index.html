Tourist visas are non-immigrant visas granted to persons who want to enter a certain country temporarily for business, leisure, or a combination of both. With this visa, the duration of your stay in a certain country is limited depending on the duration approval of the Embassy Consul.

A student visa is a non-immigrant visa that allows foreign students to enter and stay in their preferred country overseas for the duration of their studies or coursework. A student visa is special. Before applying for a visa, students are required to be enrolled at a qualified educational institution. Student visas are issued for the period it takes the student to complete his/her course of study, program, or work assignment. The course of study and the type of institution determines the category of visa that will be issued to the student.

This visa enables the dependents of a person who is a citizen or permanent resident of a certain country to enter and remain within the aforementioned country. The legal resident, otherwise referred to as the sponsor, is responsible for the visa application.

This visa allows graduates to stay and work in that country for a certain period after graduation to gain professional experience in their field of study.
